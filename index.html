Celebrating Holy Mass

We are a growing community, gathering at St. Aloysius church, West Allis, every Sundays at 2 PM to worship God through Malayalam Mass. If you are a Malayalee living around Milwaukee area you are welcome to join us. Our dream is to build up a strong faithful malayalee community, here in Milwaukee. With the intention of helping our children in knowing our faith, culture and language we do plan different programs for the coming academic year.

Building a stronger community

We gather to celebrate feasts, sacraments, holidays, and other memorable occasions. Our picnics and religious tours strengthen our community.


Sunday School

Before Mass Sunday, starting at 3 PM kids gather to learn and grow in Jesus. Kids learn prayers, songs and do group activities to enrich their spiritual life.
